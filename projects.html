Introduction

In my final year of studies, I conducted my FYP with the topic "Integrate aquarium with AI and IoT for STEAM Education". This project is supported by a local company - Winstars Enterprise HK Limited (Winstars), and a local secondary school - Stewards MKMCF Ma Ko Pan Memorial College (MKP). It aims to integrate the concept of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and the Internet of Things (IoT) into a fish tank, which will then be used to facilitate and promote STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics) teaching at schools.
